Dental Care

Dental department available facilities:

  • A dental department equipped with state-of-the-art equipment
  • Removal of tobacco/gutkha betel nut stains on teeth.
  • Root Canal Treatment (Single sitting root canal treatment services available)
  • Diagnosis and treatment of all diseases related to persistent mouth
  • Treatment of bleeding or pus from the gums (like thrush)
  • Digital X-ray (RVG)
  • Dental implants, metal, ceramic, zirconia, etc. on teeth. Fitting the cap
  • Biopsy service available by removing a piece of red/white spot on cheek, tongue
  • Wisdom teeth removal Crazy crooked teeth straightening.
  • Placement of implants, permanent placement of eyebrows with the help of implants.
  • Fractures of facial bones or jaws (all surgeries for broken jaws in road accidents and very fine suturing of facial wounds)
  • Diagnosis and treatment of diseases that make it difficult to open the mouth, as well as thorough and complete guidance for teeth and eyes
